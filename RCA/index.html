Biography :

Raja Club Athletic (RCA) is a football club in morocco espacially the city of Casablanca founded in 20/03/1949 exactely in Derb Sultan one of the the famous and oldest neighborhood in casablanca . the Club was created as form of rebellion and resistance against the colonisation of france at that time toward morocco.

Founded in 1949 by a group of Moroccan resistance fighters and nationalists, Raja Club Athlétique quickly became a symbol of hope and pride for the people of Casablanca and Morocco we montions few of the founders of this club : Hamidou El Watani, Laâchfoubi El Bouazzaoui, Boujemaâ Kadri, Mahjoub Ben Seddik and more .

In Derb Sultan and especially in a coffe shop called (al-watan) the founders of Raja club athletic (Raja casablanca) used to hold their regroupement and discuss their planes .The name of Raja camed after a long discussion about the name and each suggest a name ; until Si Kaddour(one of many founders) said the phrase (الرجاء في الله) means "our hope is in god" while "الرجاء = Hope" most of the members liked the name and agreed on it .

History and trophies :

The Green Eagles are considered one of the best teams not only in Morocco but in Africa, ranking 5th in Africa in terms of international trophies.

International trophies:

  • CAF Champions League: 3 titles (1989, 1997, 1999)
  • CAF Confederation Cup: 2 titles (2018, 2021)
  • CAF Super Cup: 2 titles (2000, 2019)
  • CAF Cup: 1 title (defunct competition) (2003)
  • Afro-Asian Cup: 1 title (1998)

National trophies:

Nationally RCA is one of the best teams in morocco if it's not the best. since historically Raja Casablanca has more head-to-head wins than any other Moroccan club in direct one-on-one matchups—this dominance holds true across all their rivals, including Wydad AC and every other major team in Morocco.

  • Moroccan League (Botola Pro): 13 titles (1988, 1996, 1997, 1998, 1999, 2001, 2004, 2009, 2011, 2013, 2020, 2021, 2024)
  • Moroccan Throne Cup: 9 titles (1974, 1977, 1982, 1996, 2002, 2005, 2012, 2017, 2023)

Regional trophies:

  • Arab Club Champions Cup: 2 titles (2006, 2020)
  • North African Club Cup: 1 title (2015)

Adding to these impressive titles, Raja Casablanca was not only the first Moroccan club to participate in the FIFA Club World Cup in 2000 but also made history by reaching the final in 2013. They remain the only African club to ever reach the final and the first Arab club to achieve this milestone, after remarkable victories over top teams like Atlético Mineiro and Monterrey.

Fans and supporters :

Raja Casablanca’s fans, known as the 'Green Eagles,' are widely regarded as the most passionate and dedicated supporters in Moroccan and African football.Being the biggest fan base in morocco and one of the biggest in africa the supporters choosed the south place if the complex stadium Mohammed 5 as there base and often called it (magana) , The Curva sud are famous for their electrifying chants, mesmerizing tifos, and unwavering presence in every match, they transform stadiums into seas of green. Their loyalty transcends victories and defeats, creating an unbreakable bond between the club and its supporters. Among Moroccan fans, the saying goes: 'كل الفرق لها جمهور، والرجاء لها شعب' — meaning, 'Every club has fans, but Raja has a nation.' This spirit of unity and pride has made the Raja faithful a symbol of football passion across the world.
